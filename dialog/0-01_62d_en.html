Quest:
After injuring a Nightshade, use [Lesser Heal] to deal the final blow and obtain 1 Cleansed Soul Crystal for Gullveig.

Guide:
Nightshades are on 2nd Floor of Promise Island Dungeon.
Gullveig (32674,32830) is at the House of Magic in Southern Promise Island.

Description:
Lesser Heal is a basic magic spell that can heal and cleanse a monster's corrupt soul. Gullveig ask you to practice cleansing with the Lesser Heal spell on a Nightshade's Soul Crystal. Collect a cleansed Nightshade's Soul Crystal and bring it back to Gullveig.
