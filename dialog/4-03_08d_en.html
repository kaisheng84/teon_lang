Quest:
Bring 2 Thri Kreen hearts to Tiago.

Guide:
Bewitched Thri Kreens are in Maros Dungeon.
Tiago (32839,33541) [Maros Town] is in Northwestern Maros Town.

Description:
Tiago wants to study how the dead souls are bewitching the Thri Kreen in Maros Dungeon. He needs you to gather 2 Thri Kreen hearts for him to do so and will give you a Void Stone for them.
