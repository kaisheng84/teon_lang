Quest:
Rescue Nat.

Guide:
Nat (Kill Skeletons to get the coordinates) is somewhere in the Promise Island Dungeon.

Description:
Owen and his friend Nat were attacked while there were in the Dungeon by skeletons. Owen escaped, but Nat is still down there. Defeat the Skeletons and look for clues to where Nat might be.
(Look for Skeletons, Skeleton Spearmen, and Skeleton Axeman).
