Quest:
Collect 10 Bone Fragments for Karol.

Guide:
Skeletons are all over the dungeons. Their bone fragments are commonly used in crafting.
Karol (32813,33551) [Maros Town] is in Center of Maros Town.

Description:
Karol is looking to invoke an ancient magical barrier to protect the town, but needs you to collect Bone Fragments to create it. They can be found inside most Dungeons.
