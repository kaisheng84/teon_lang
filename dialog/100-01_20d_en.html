Quest:
Hunt Weakened Owlbears and take 3 pieces of Weakened Owlbear Meat to the Orc.

Guide:
Weaken Owlbears are found on Sky Island to the Southwest.
The Orc (32693,32762) [Sky Island] is to the Southwest.

Description:
A cowardly Orc cannot return home unless he goes back with Weakened Owlbear meat. Helping him with this task so he can leave return and unblock the path for humans.
