Quest:
Kill the Hobgoblins and collect 2 broken blades for Rhoda.

Guide:
Hobgoblins are near the Southern Crossroads of Promise Island Town.
Rhoda (32736,32743) [House in Southern Promise Island Town] is in a House in Southern Promise Island Town.

Description:
Rhoda is willing to get back into the fight with you, but needs a weapon. He has asked you to collect broken blades from Hobgoblins for him to forge a new weapon for himself.
