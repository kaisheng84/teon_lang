Quest:
Kill the Lycanthrope and collect 2 Pelts for the soldier guarding Cassandra's Home.

Guide:
The Lycanthrope is to the Southeast of Cassandra's Home.
The soldier guarding Cassandra's Home (32803,32777) [Eastern Promise Island] is just outside Promise Island Town, to the East.



Description:
To enter the cave to Cassandra's Home, you must kill the Lycanthropes and collect their pelts for the solder that guards the entrance.
