Quest:
Kill the Stone Golem, use a Scroll of Resurrection on it, then kill it again and report back to Cora.

Guide:
Stone Golems can be found all over Promise Island.
Cora (32737,32722) is near a house in Western Promise Island village.

Description:
Cora would like to find out if a Scroll of Resurrection will work on a Stone Golem. She has asked you to perform the experiment. Kill a Stone Golem and then use the Scroll of Resurrection to bring it back from the dead.
