Quest:
Hunt Weakened Harpies and get 4 feathers for Ambrose.

Guide:
Weakened Harpies are found on Southern Sky Island, near the Edin Monument.
Ambrose (32695,32814) [Edin Monument] is at the Edin Monument in Southern Sky Island.

Description:
Ambrose has sworn an oath to guard the secrets of the Edin Monument. He asks you to help him retrieve Weakened Harpy feathers that will allow him to fight winged attackers in the air.
