Quest:
Bring the Heavy Metal to the Armor Specialist.

Guide:
Armor Specialist (32777,32578) is in the Northwestern corner of Promise Island.

Description:
Bran has ordered everyone in Promise Island Town to give up all their possession made of metal in order to forge heavy armor for his men, but it's not enough. Andrew has given you a small amount of good forging metal in hopes it will quell Bran's anger. Bring the good forging metal to the Armor Specialist.
