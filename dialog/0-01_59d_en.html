Quest:
Kill Skeleton Mages and collect 1 Skeletal Mage Bone Marrow for Owen.

Guide:
The Skeletal Mages are in the 2nd Floor of Promise Island Dungeon.
Owen (32781,32784) is to the Southeast of Promise Island Town.

Description:
Nat has been bitten by a Zombie and will turn soon! He was in the Dungeon with Owen to look for a cure for his curse. To save Owen, collect a Skeleton Mage Bone Marrow and give it to Owen.
